What is Polymarket?

How It Works

1. Market Prices = Probabilities
$0.71 per share means the market thinks there's a 71% chance Arsenal wins.

2. Buy Shares Based on Research
If you think the market is wrong (based on your analysis), you can profit from that edge.

3. Shares Pay $1.00 If Correct
When the event resolves, correct predictions receive $1.00 per share. Wrong predictions get $0.00.
